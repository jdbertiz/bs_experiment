Typography

Our typography remains rooted in the bold modernity of Graphik, our primary typeface. The addition of GT Sectra Fine, a contemporary serif typeface, provides an aspect of warmth and individuality to our messaging.

Accenture brand guidelines

Primary typeface
Graphik

Our primary typeface is Graphik, a rationalist type system with low contrast and a large x-height, making it highly versatile for display, text, captions and UI/UX design. Ensure a clear distinction between weights by using a strong contrast.

Basic type hierarchy:
golden ratio

Having distinctly different levels of type hierarchy makes text easier to read and navigate. Each level should be noticeably bigger than the one below it. In this first example, we use the golden ratio scale to achieve this. In addition to this ratio, you can also use other things like color and weight to make the hierarchy clearer.

GT Sectra has been intentionally omitted from this example. Its use should be limited to call-outs and quotes.

Headlines should always be in sentence case unless the title is proprietary
